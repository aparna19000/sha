Once upon there lives a monkey on a jamoon tree on the banks of a river. Also in the same river there lived 
a crocodile and his wife.


One day the crocodile was travelling a long distance and searching for food, and then he saw the monkey
on the jamoon tree. crocodile asked for monkey to give his some food. 

Kind monkey offered him some fruits, the crocodile was delighted to eat jamoons and he was very 
thankful to the monkey. Also he asked him if he could visit monkey again. monkey agreed.
